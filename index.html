Business Analysis & EQ Coaching

Elevate Your Analysis & Leadership

Bridging the gap between technical execution and human connection.

My Coaching Philosophy

Delivering successful projects isn't just about documenting requirements; it's about understanding the people behind them. I believe that the best professionals possess a dual-threat capability: sharp technical acumen and deep emotional intelligence.

Whether you are a Business Analyst looking to master Agile delivery and documentation, or a leader striving to build psychological safety and conduct high-impact 1-on-1s, my coaching provides the exact frameworks you need to turn data into decisions and conversations into growth.

Coaching Tracks

Track A: Business Analysis Mastery

Navigate the modern BA landscape with confidence, from foundational techniques to Agile ceremonies.

  • Agile Delivery: Master the art of writing testable User Stories, defining Acceptance Criteria, and leading Backlog Refinements.
  • Documentation Clarity: Understand exactly when and how to use a BRD (the "What"), an FRD (the "How"), and an SRS (the Blueprint).
  • Core Techniques: Apply the 10 essential BA tools, including Root Cause Analysis (5 Whys), Process Mapping, and MoSCoW Prioritization.
  • Stakeholder Alignment: Prevent bottlenecks by mastering the RACI Matrix (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed).

Track B: Emotional Intelligence (EQ) & Leadership

Develop the soft skills necessary to lead with empathy, calm the busy mind, and foster genuine connection.

  • Empathetic Communication: Transition from "Listening to Fix" (rushing to solve) to "Listening to Connect" (creating safety and stillness).
  • Overcoming the Busy Mind: Identify and dismantle cognitive traps like "The Fortune Teller" and "The Judge" to build professional resilience.
  • The EQ Coach Approach: Structure powerful 1-on-1s using the 3-step Engage, Activate, and Reflect framework.
  • The Wheel of Coaching: Guide your team from defining their current state to exploring open questions and practicing actionable next steps.
