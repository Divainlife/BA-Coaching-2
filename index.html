The ALIGN Framework

The Wheeler Fortune signature coaching model guiding clients through Awareness, Learning, Implementation, Growth, and Navigation.

A — Awareness

Gain clarity on your current state.

L — Learning

Build BA knowledge and tools.

I — Implementation

Apply new skills through guided exercises.

G — Growth

Strengthen your confidence and impact.

N — Navigation

Plan your next steps strategically.
