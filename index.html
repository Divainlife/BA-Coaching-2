Brand Promise: Structured clarity, aligned action, and measurable progress.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: Empower individuals to gain clarity, align their actions with their goals, and build sustainable progress through structured coaching.

Vision: To become a trusted coaching practice known for operational clarity, personal alignment, and transformative growth.

Coaching Philosophy

Clarity & Alignment Coaching™ is grounded in ethical, structured, client‑centered support that empowers clients to make aligned decisions and achieve measurable progress.

The ALIGN Method™

  1. A — Assess current state
  2. L — Locate misalignment
  3. I — Identify goals
  4. G — Generate action steps
  5. N — Navigate progress

8‑Week BA Coaching Program Schedule

Week 1 — Foundations & Clarity

Program onboarding, BA competency assessment, clarity mapping, and goal alignment.

Week 2 — Requirements Mastery

Business requirements vs. functional requirements, elicitation techniques, and requirement structuring.

Week 3 — Process Mapping

Workflow analysis, BPMN fundamentals, swimlane diagrams, and process optimization.

Week 4 — Stakeholder Engagement

Stakeholder analysis, communication strategies, conflict navigation, and influence techniques.

Week 5 — Agile & Scrum BA

User stories, acceptance criteria, backlog refinement, and Agile ceremonies.

Week 6 — Technical BA Skills

API literacy, data mapping, systems thinking, and integration fundamentals.

Week 7 — Testing & Traceability

UAT planning, test case design, RTM development, and defect triage.

Week 8 — Career Strategy & Portfolio

Resume refinement, BA portfolio creation, interview coaching, and certification planning.
