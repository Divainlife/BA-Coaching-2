About Wheeler Fortune Coaching

Wheeler Fortune Business Support Consultants is a minority-owned coaching and support hub based in New Jersey. Our BA Coaching division helps individuals transition into Business Analyst roles through structured learning, hands-on tools, and personalized mentorship.

We serve minority and women-owned startups, first-time business owners, immigrants entering professional roles, and individuals seeking career advancement.
